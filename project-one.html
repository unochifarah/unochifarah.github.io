Executive Summary

SaaS Profitability &
Discount Optimization Dashboard

This project involves the development of a comprehensive sales and profitability dashboard designed for a global SaaS provider. By analyzing 10,000+ transactions across multiple regions, the dashboard identifies high-potential growth areas while flagging sectors where aggressive discounting is eroding profit margins.

Decision Impact Regional Discount Policy Revision
Adoption 95% Org-wide

The Problem

While the organization saw consistent growth in total sales, net profitability was inconsistent across different geographies. Specifically, the APJ (Asia-Pacific & Japan) region was showing significant losses in high-volume sectors like Tech and Healthcare, raising concerns about the sustainability of current discounting strategies.

Critical Insights & Actions

The Discovery

The APJ Tech sector was identified as the most unprofitable segment (over $5,000 in losses), driven by discounts often exceeding 40%. The Finance and Energy industries in EMEA/AMER contributed over 35% of total profits with minimal discount reliance.

The Decision

Replaced flat-rate discount permissions with a "Dynamic Margin Guardrail" that requires management approval for any discount that results in a negative profit. Reallocated 20% of the regional marketing budget to double down on these high-margin "Core" sectors.

Operational Dashboard

This dashboard enabled leadership to monitor carrier performance in real-time and validate the rerouting strategy within the first 14 days of implementation.

Decision Support Tool

Figure 1.0: Geographic Heatmap showing carrier-specific lag spikes during peak surge hours. This view was used in daily 15-minute ops standups.

Geographic Drill-Down Logic

Users can click on a region (e.g., EMEA) to instantly see the specific cities and customers driving the most (or least) profit.

Automated Margin Alerting

A real-time alerting system that flags any "Strategic" segment order where the profit margin falls below the 15% corporate benchmark.

High-Adoption UX

A simplified "Executive Summary" view that translates complex financial data into three color-coded KPIs: Growth, Margin Health, and Discount Intensity.

Approach

01

Data Engineering & Profitability Modeling

Cleaned and transformed raw sales data to calculate true profit margins, allowing for a side-by-side comparison of "Gross Sales" vs "Net Profit" across 10+ industries.

02

Segmented Performance Analysis

Performed a deep dive into the "SMB" vs "Strategic" segments to determine if high-volume small business sales were actually contributing to the bottom line after discounts were applied.

Reflection

Prioritizing Profitability over Volume This project highlighted that high sales volume can be deceptive; without monitoring margins, growth in certain regions was actually costing the company money.

Localization of Pricing Learned that a "one-size-fits-all" global discount policy is ineffective, as market conditions in APJ required a more nuanced, value-based pricing approach compared to EMEA.
